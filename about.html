The Founders

Custodians of Ancient History

Born from the ancient sandstone formations of the Cederberg region, Donkieskraal is more than a lodge; it is a living museum. Our journey began with a deep respect for the land and a desire to preserve its rugged beauty for generations to come. We believe in minimal intervention, allowing the dramatic rock sculptures and indigenous fynbos to take center stage.
